About Me

I'm Kaviya Sekar, an Electrical & Computer Engineer specializing in VLSI design and hardware validation. Currently pursuing my MS at Binghamton University while working as a Graduate Research Assistant on pre-silicon validation for complex SoCs and AI chips.

Alongside my academic research, I work as a Test Engineer at Sanmina Corporation in San Jose, CA — developing ICT test programs, improving production yield, and resolving hardware-level failures across high-speed manufacturing environments.

I bridge theoretical hardware design with real-world manufacturing reliability — combining VLSI, SystemVerilog, and embedded systems knowledge with hands-on test engineering expertise.
